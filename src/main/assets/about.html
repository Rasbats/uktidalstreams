Times of High Water

Times of high water are calculated using harmonic constants from http://www.flaterco.com/ . These may differ
significantly from official sources. The app does not replace official publications and should be used with caution.

The code to calculate HW times comes from TidePredictor, a free app by Paul Lupus available on Google Play.
HW times are adjusted for BST (End of March to end of October).


Obtaining set and rate of the tidal current

Move the cursor to the base of a tidal arrow to see the set and rate of the tidal current at that arrow.
